Tinkaria Mark Variations

Exploring dark orange/red integration into the logo mark

Current (fine)

Dark green strokes, small salmon center dot. Minimal color presence.

V1 — Ember Ring

Circle stroke becomes warm red. Spokes stay dark. Bolder center dot. Subtle brand presence.

V2 — Full Blaze

All strokes in deep warm red. Bold, monochromatic warmth on cream.

V3 — Dark Ember

Dark background with warm red strokes. Reversed treatment, glowing feel. Good for dark mode.

V4 — Gradient Core

Radial gradient: warm red at center fading to dark at tips. Organic depth.

V5 — Warm Duo

Two-layer spokes: outer tips in red, inner segments dark. Ring in red. Layered depth effect.

V6 — Cardinal Accent

Cardinal spokes (N/S/E/W) in red, diagonals in dark. Ring stays dark. Directional emphasis.
